Detailed Description

Apply a Manual Scale-Correction to all wood/stone/ice-rectangleblocks in the scene. By empirical research we found that the block-sizes in the game are always certain values ( which are stored in the sizeIntervalLUT ). (Empirical means here: making screenshots of the game, measuring pixels and the calculating). The modifier makes sure that the scale of those entities is the closest of those empirical values. WARNING 1: THIS IS AN ASSUMPTION AND MAY BE WRONG (although so far it seems to work out pretty well). WARNING 2: THE VALUES MAY NOT BE COMPLETE OR DISPROVEN by further empircal evidence.
